## 🚀 Featured Projects

### 🔹 [Restaurant Platfrom](https://github.com/NaumVlavceski/Restaurant-Platform)
> A mobile-first ordering platform where customers scan a QR code at their table to browse the menu and place orders directly — no waiting for a waiter. Reduces service bottlenecks and lets staff handle more tables simultaneously.
> `Python` `Django` `React` `TailwindCSS` `PostgreSQL` [ · Live Demo ↗](https://restorantp.netlify.app/)

### 🔹 [Bookenda](https://github.com/NaumVlavceski/Bookenda)
> An event management platform where anyone can create public events and sell tickets to attendees. Simple, straightforward, and open to everyone — no gatekeeping, just events and people. 
> `Python` `Django` `Bootstrap` `PostgreSQL`

### 🔹 [SmartPark](https://mk-smart-parking.onrender.com)
> A smart city parking reservation system that lets drivers claim a designated parking spot in advance, guaranteeing their space before they even leave home. Built collaboratively as a student capstone project. 
> `Python` `Django` `React` `TailwindCSS` `Docker` `PostgreSQL` `GitHub` `Jira`[ · Live Demo ↗](https://mk-smart-parking.onrender.com)

### 🔹 [Child System](https://github.com/NaumVlavceski/ChildSystem)
> A role-based child assessment platform connecting parents, therapists, and admins. Parents submit developmental forms for their children, therapists review and score them — streamlining the evaluation process in one organized system.
> `Python` `Django` `Bootstrap` `PostgreSQL`
